    • How to prepare O Bendito Pão de Queijo
    • Remove the product from the freezer only just before baking (from the freezer to the oven)
    • Place the cheese rolls with a 2cm gap
    • Preheat oven for about 15 minutes at 180°C
    • Bake for approx. 30 min
    • Remove from the oven when the cheese roll is golden brown
