About us

And why we are so passionate about cheese breads!


How we began

In 2003, A family from São Paulo moved to Minas Gerais, discovering a world of gastronomic traditions. This special taste for cooking has always existed in the Romero Garcia family, but it was in Minas Gerais that the passion for gastronomy was unleashed.

Mr. Ageu and his family moved back to São Paulo, and in 2014 they began selling their cheese rolls, in a homemade style, applying all the traditions brought from the farms of Minas Gerais. This way they created O Bendito Pão de Queijo, the traditional Brazilian cheese bread.



Healthy and tasteful

O Bendito Pão de Queijo is made with 100% natural Non-GMO ingredients and milk from cows not treated with rBST. The recipe is unique and very traditional. There are three types of organic Brazilian cheese in the recipe, totaling 40% of the final product.

This famous cheese bread has been enjoyed in Brazil since 2014, and now it is available to you. It's perfect for making meat and cheese sliders or dipping in your favorite sauce. You can have the orginal taste of Brazil in your table.
