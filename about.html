How we began

A family from Sao Paulo moved to Minas Gerais, discovering a world of gastronomic traditions. This taste for cooking has always existed in the Garcia family, but it was in Minas Gerais that the passion for gastronomy was touched.
In 2015 Ageu Garcia and his family moved back to Sao Paulo and began selling her cheese rolls, in a homemade style, applying all the traditions brought from the farms of Minas Gerais. They created O Bendito Pão de Queijo, the traditional Brazilian cheese bread.

Healthy and tasteful

O Bendito Pão de Queijo is made with 100% natural ingredients, with no gluten, soy and sugar free. The recipe is unique and very traditional. The cheese is the main ingredient, with NON GMO.

Based on the family homemade recipe, the famous cheese bread has been enjoyed in Brazil since 2015, and now is available for you. Perfect for making meat and cheese sliders or dipping in your favorite sauce. You can have the orginal taste of Brazil in your table. Contact us.
